# Iteration Planning Meeting (IPM)

At the start of each iteration, we hold an Iteration Planning Meeting (often
referred to as an IPM). The aim of the meeting is to ensure that there is enough
prioritised work at the top of the backlog to be worked on for the next
iteration.

At iteration planning meeting normally takes about **1 hour**.

The prioritisation of the work is lead by the _Product Owner_. They bring the
work into the and backlog and the team reviews it.

There are a few steps which need to be performed for each story:

1. Check that it has met the requirements of the _Definition of Ready (DoR)_
2. Discuss what's involved to deliver this story (does the team have enough
   information?)
3. Update the estimates (this can be done with a round of
   [Planning Poker](https://www.mountaingoatsoftware.com/agile/planning-poker)).
